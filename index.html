Resident Evaluation System

"RES" is an evaluation system for doctorial students working in conjuction with professors at the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga and Doctors at Erlanger Hospital in downtown Chattanooga(UTC).

This app utilizes Firebase to persist and distribute requests from students to instructors and vice versa.

I produced all logo's and images used inside the app, maintain its functionality and work alongside the users to provide features which make their lives easier!

Concussed?

"Concussed" is a short medical app designed to allow users to perform the Flankter Test on the field of sport play.

This app resolves the issue of needing to schedule appointments long after a concussion has occured. It is backed by medical research performed by doctors at UTC and Erlanger.

Autism Environment

Those who may be recognized as Autistic, or living with autism symptoms, are known to commonly experience a phenomenon where-by their brain pathways associate pain with uncomfortable sensory patterns.

This app is an educational experience, based on scientific research, with videos and games to educate through experience.
